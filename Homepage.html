The Problem

Gauteng's industries lose significant time and money due to traffic delays, poor route planning, and unpredictable congestion. Delivery trucks often get stuck in traffic, which delays goods reaching factories, warehouses, or customers. This increases fuel costs, late deliveries, and customer dissatisfaction.

Our AI Solution

Our system uses machine learning, time series analysis, and deep learning to predict traffic patterns and optimize delivery routes in real-time. This reduces costs for businesses, improves delivery speed, and benefits the municipality through reduced traffic congestion and lower carbon emissions.
